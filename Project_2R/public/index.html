About

FitTrain is a simple app that will match clients and trainers together in their area.
The app will guide the inital trainer and client relationship.

  • Match Clients with Trainers

    We will match you up with the right trainer and clients. Our simple easy to use signup will find the perfect fit.

  • Clients and trainers setup workout plans

    Clients and trainers will evaluate workout goals together during inital meeting. Our easy to use platform will guide client and trainer through the process. Inital clients stats will be taken and inputed into the platform for tracking and accountability.

  • Put plan into action -
    start training

    Let the fun begin, client and trainers meet on a regular process to excute workout plan. Our system will keep track of appointments, send calender reminders and allow for updates.

  • Track, measure and tweak client progress

    Our easy platform will help keep track of all the client's stats. If something isn't record our graphs and charts will help identify the problem and make recommendations for improvement.
